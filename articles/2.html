Letter From Sucessful Adoptee

by Karen

While Manny still plays aggressively (my feet under the covers are fair game!) he's comfortable enough to jump in the bed when I call him and, of his own accord, nudges me for pets.? He sleeps beside me, flat on his back like a dog, for most of the night. In the wee hours he still needs to ramp it up and find something to amuse him... the spoon we shared yogurt with earlier has wound up in the bed...as do most of the toys.? I've learned to allow extra time in the morning when I'm in the bathroom because he loves water. Turn a faucet on... he's there! Never had a cat that did that before.

Thank you again for the wonderful work you do... We wouldn't have found each other without you!
