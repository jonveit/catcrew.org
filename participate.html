How You Can Help

  • Cat transport to spay/neuter clinic
  • Feeding stations
  • Distribute information to community
  • Donations of canned food, dry food, cat toys
  • Fostering of cats/kittens until they are ready for adoption
  • Volunteering at adoption events or fundraising event
  • Carriers, dog crates, traps that you are no longer using

Simply contact us if you're at all able to help! Thanks!
